Matcha is a type of green tea that comes in a powder form, has an earthy taste, and provides you with great health benefits! For example, matcha can help with focus, stress, and sleep!

Photo Credit: Just One CookBook

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp matcha powder
  • 1 tbsp hot water
  • 1/2 cup oat milk
  • 1 tsp agave/honey

Steps

  1. Put the matcha powder in a mug or small bowl
  2. Add in the hot water
  3. Whisk the matcha until there are no lumps (use an M shape motion when whisking.)
  4. Add in the oat milk and stir to mix the matcha and oat milk together. You could also use a frother for this step.
  5. Add the agave/honey and stir again. Enjoy!
