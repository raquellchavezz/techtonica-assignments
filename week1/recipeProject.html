Yummy Matcha Latte 🍵🐸🌱🌿

Matcha is a type of green tea that comes in a powder form, an earthy taste, and has great health benifits! For example, matcha can help with focus, stress, and sleep.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp matcha powder
  • 1 tbsp hot water
  • 1/2 cup oat milk
  • 1 tsp agave/honey

Steps

  1. Put the matcha powder in a mug or small bowl
  2. Add in the hot water
  3. Whisk the matcha until there are no lumps (use M shape motion when whisking)
  4. Add in the oat milk and stir to mix the matcha and oat milk together
  5. Add the agave/honey and stir again. Enjoy!
